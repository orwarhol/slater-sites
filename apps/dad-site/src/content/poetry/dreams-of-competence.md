---
title: "Dreams of Competence"
date: 2005-01-07
tags: ["Aging","Little Poems"]
excerpt: "Quick. Thorough. Mood modulated. Effective like the old days. Steady on my feet; able to see the end. Pity it’s only a dream."
---

Quick.  
Thorough.  
Mood modulated.  
Effective like the old days.  
Steady on my feet; able to see the end.  
Pity it’s only a dream.
